Germany’s Kroos to retire from football after Euro 20 | DNBC News

By DNBC News | 21 May 2024

Toni Kroos, one of football’s most esteemed midfielders, has announced that he will retire from professional football following the UEFA Euro 2024 tournament. This decision marks the end of an illustrious career spanning nearly two decades, during which Kroos has been a key player for both his club teams and the German national team. His departure will undoubtedly be felt across the football world, as he has been a central figure in many memorable moments and significant victories.​

Kroos began his professional journey with Bayern Munich, where he quickly rose through the ranks to become one of the most promising young talents in German football. His time at Bayern was marked by domestic dominance, including multiple Bundesliga titles and a UEFA Champions League triumph in 2013. His performances at Bayern laid the foundation for his reputation as a midfielder with exceptional vision, precision, and composure on the ball.

In 2014, Kroos made a high-profile move to Real Madrid, where he continued to excel and further solidify his status as one of the world’s best midfielders. At Real Madrid, Kroos was instrumental in the team’s success, helping them secure numerous Champions League titles and other domestic and international honors. His ability to control the tempo of the game and deliver pinpoint passes made him an invaluable asset to the team.

Kroos’ impact was not limited to club football. He has been a mainstay in the German national team, earning over 100 caps since his debut in 2010. His international career is highlighted by Germany's victory in the 2014 FIFA World Cup, where his performances were critical to their success. Kroos’ contributions in major tournaments have consistently showcased his ability to perform at the highest level under immense pressure.

One of the defining aspects of Kroos’ playing style is his exceptional passing accuracy and vision. He has been renowned for his ability to dictate play from midfield, making him a pivotal playmaker for his teams. His composure and tactical intelligence have allowed him to excel in various midfield roles, whether as a deep-lying playmaker or in a more advanced position. Kroos’ technical skills and understanding of the game have set him apart as a player who can influence the outcome of matches significantly.

Kroos’ decision to retire after Euro 2024 stems from a desire to spend more time with his family and explore interests beyond football. While his departure will be a significant loss for both Real Madrid and the German national team, it also marks the end of a chapter that has been filled with remarkable achievements and unforgettable moments. His retirement will leave a void that will be challenging to fill, both on and off the pitch.

As he prepares to hang up his boots, Kroos leaves behind a legacy that will be remembered for years to come. His career is a testament to his dedication, skill, and professionalism. He has inspired countless young players with his performances and has set a high standard for what it means to be a world-class midfielder. Kroos’ contributions to football have been immense, and his presence in the sport will be deeply missed.
